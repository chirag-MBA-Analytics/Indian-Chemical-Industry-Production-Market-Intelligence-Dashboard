# Indian-Chemical-Industry-Production-Market-Intelligence-Dashboard


## Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing production trends, market intelligence, and industrial growth patterns in the Indian chemical industry using SQL and Power BI. The dashboard was developed to transform large-scale industrial production data into actionable business insights related to pricing trends, production concentration, market growth, and category-level performance.

The project combines chemical industry understanding with business analytics and market intelligence concepts to identify production patterns and support strategic decision-making.



## Objectives

* Analyze chemical production trends across multiple product categories
* Identify high-growth and low-growth chemical segments
* Build market intelligence dashboards for industrial analysis
* Perform Year-over-Year (YoY) production growth analysis
* Study category-wise production concentration and market patterns
* Translate industrial datasets into business insights using SQL and Power BI

---

## Dataset Information

* Source: Indian chemical production datasets integrated with AMAI and Assochem industry research
* Time Period: 2013–2020
* Scale: 85K+ MT production data
* Coverage:

  * 91 chemical products
  * 5 major chemical categories

## Key Analysis Performed

### 1. Production Trend Analysis

* Evaluated annual production changes across chemical categories
* Identified high-growth and declining production periods
* Analyzed industry fluctuations including COVID-19 impact trends

### 2. Market Intelligence Analysis

* Compared production contribution across chemical segments
* Studied category concentration and production distribution
* Identified major production-driving chemical groups

### 3. Year-over-Year Growth Analysis

* Calculated YoY growth trends using SQL window functions
* Measured growth consistency across products and categories
* Built dynamic growth tracking visuals in Power BI

### 4. Segmentation Analysis

* Categorized products into High / Medium / Low production segments
* Developed comparative dashboards for segment-wise analysis
* Evaluated production contribution patterns
